Courtesy Rules
  1. Smoking is not permitted on Metropolitan Cummuter Railroad trains.
  2. No seats are resevered. Please do not inconvenience other customers by holding seats or blocking seats with parcels or wraps.
  3. Please refrain from placing feet on seats or upper deck railings.
  4. Please do not block the doors, making it difficult for passengers to board or disembark at their stations.
  5. Please be considerate of others and keep volume on head sets low.
  6. Help us to maintain a clean environment by not leaving litter on trains, in stations, or in stairwells and walkways.
  7. Passengers whose conduct is disorderly or unsafe will not be allowed on the train.
  8. Obscene language is prohibited.
